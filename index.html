PHASE 01

Qualifiers

48 squads split across 4 lobbies. Points from placement and eliminations across 3 matches. Top 3 squads per lobby advance.

PHASE 02

Semifinals

12 surviving squads, 2 lobbies, 3 matches each. Highest combined score per lobby moves on to the grand finale.

PHASE 03

Grand Finale

6 squads, single lobby, best of 3 on rotating maps. Highest total score across all matches wins Booyah X .

// Core Rules

Read Before
You Drop

Full ruleset is sent to captains on confirmation. These are the essentials.

01

Fair play only

No emulators on mobile-only lobbies, no third-party software, no teaming outside your registered squad. Violations mean instant disqualification.

02

Check-in window

Squads must check in within 30 minutes of their lobby's start time. No-shows forfeit that match's points.

03

Scoring

Placement points plus 1 point per elimination. Ties are broken by total eliminations, then by best single-match placement.

04

Disputes

Any scoring or conduct dispute must be raised within 15 minutes of match end via the tournament admin channel.
